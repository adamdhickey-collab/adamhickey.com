Overview

For eight years at Pixel Farm, I crafted interactive websites and apps that brought warmth to the screen, turning corporate ideas into human experiences through storytelling and motion.

The internet is a noisy place. Everyone is shouting. Our job was to be the ones whispering something interesting in the corner. We had to take big, corporate ideas and make them feel human. Sometimes we succeeded. Sometimes we just made really nice looking buttons. But we always tried to tell a story.
